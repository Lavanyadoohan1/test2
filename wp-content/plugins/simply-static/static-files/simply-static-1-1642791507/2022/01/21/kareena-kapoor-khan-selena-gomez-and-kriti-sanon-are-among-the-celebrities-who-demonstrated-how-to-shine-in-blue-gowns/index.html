Kareena Kapoor Khan, Selena Gomez, and Kriti Sanon are among the celebrities who demonstrated how to shine in blue gowns.

ByAdmin

Jan 21, 2022

The number of virtual parties has recently increased. After all, it’s a soothing remedy that instantly transforms a bad day into a good one. Let’s introduce you to some party glam to get you in the mood to dance. So, are we teasing you with a selection of dresses that seem enticingly appropriate? Indeed, there’s so much to love about a sparkly dress that you only need a few accessories to complete your party look.

Say goodbye to dull days because we have deep shades of blue to keep you looking pretty. So, let’s see how bright a diamond you can be when you hit the dance floor with your friends. We learned from celebrities who looked stunning in blue gowns with a lot of glitz. Here’s how to put your dress together.

Malaika Arora

This is what it looks like to discover magic in your gown. The Socha Nahin Tha chose a blue sequin gown from Nedo by Nedret Taciroglu, which featured a thigh-high slit and a beaded neckline. Make this your cocktail party outfit; it’s simply stunning. Accessorize appropriately with studded jewelry and ankle-strap stilettos.

Kareena Kapoor Khan’s

The elegantly worn thigh-high slit. Bebo is so typical of himself. Her ink blue sequin dress with shoulder pads, turtleneck, full sleeves, and a drape-like detail looked stunning here, proving she’s a sight to behold at all times. Do you want to experiment with color blocking? Hot pink strappy stilettos will give you the big vibe you’re looking for.

Meghan Markle

Here’s the day when Meghan, the pregnant former Duchess of Sussex, defined regality with her taste in fashion. She wore a shimmery three-quarter sleeve Roland Mouret gown with a boat neck and a knee-high slit. She completed her look with a black clutch and ankle-strap stilettos.

Selena Gomez’s

She walked the hot girl route for the Grammys in a sparkly plunging neckline strappy gown, as she is the pro dresser of every red carpet event. This midnight blue cut-out number looked stunning when paired with hoop earrings.

Kriti Sanon

A sequin bodycon Bardot gown that has me completely smitten. Tell us about this mid-length dress with avant-garde sleeves you sold. It combined the one-shoulder and off-shoulder details in one dress. How often do you encounter something similar? It’s a once-in-a-lifetime find. Accessorize with pink pointed-toe pumps and dangler earrings.

Priyanka Chopra’s

The stunning lady told us she’s hotter than the tropics, and we believe her. She proves it every day, and her look for the Baywatch premiere is proof of that. The 39-year-old wore a plunging neckline wrap-style dress with a thigh-high slit and a high-low hem. This full-sleeved sequin ensemble was held together by a black fabric waist belt and black ankle-strap stilettos.

Which diva’s glitzy blue gown do you want to own? Please share your thoughts in the comments section.
